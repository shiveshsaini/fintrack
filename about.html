About FinTrack

FinTrack is a personal finance tracker designed to be simple, intuitive, and powerful. Our mission is to give you a clear understanding of your spending habits so you can take control of your financial future.

The Project

This application is a capstone project built to demonstrate modern web development skills. It's created with clean HTML5, modern CSS (including Flexbox, Grid, and animations), and will be powered by JavaScript for full interactivity.

Core Features

  • Add Transactions: Quickly log your income and expenses.
  • Real-time Balance: See your total balance update instantly.
  • Visual Breakdown: A visual chart to see where your money goes.
  • Full History: A searchable, filterable log of all your transactions.
  • Local Storage: Your data is saved securely in your own browser.
